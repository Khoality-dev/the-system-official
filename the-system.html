02 — Stats & leveling

Completion avoids penalty. Quality builds you.

Everyone clears the mandatory daily — so completion can't be what grows stats. Growth comes from how well you perform, and how you perform routes to which stat.

STR / VIT / AGI

The Body stats — strength, endurance and agility. Free for everyone.

INT / PER

The Mind stats — intelligence and perception. Unlocked with Enhanced.

No allocation

Stats rise automatically at level-up, distributed by the affinity you banked.

HP = readiness

Governed by VIT. Drains on hard sessions, regens on sleep and rest — it prevents overtraining.

How growth is earned

Endurance

Duration, total volume, steady pace.

Sharpness

Accuracy, depth, focus and attention.
