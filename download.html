FAQ

Before you install.

Where should I download the app?

Use the download button on this page. Avoid copies hosted by third parties, and confirm that your browser remains on this official website before installing.

Why does Android ask to allow this installation?

Android requires confirmation before installing an APK obtained through a browser or file manager. Approve only the app you used to open this official download, then disable that permission afterward if you prefer.

What does the app access?

The app uses motion sensors during supported exercises and internet access for features that require an online connection. It does not request access to your contacts, camera, microphone, or precise location. See the privacy policy for current details.

Is my workout data uploaded?

Raw motion-sensor readings are processed on your device and are not stored or uploaded. Account and synchronized progress data are handled as described in the privacy policy.

What devices are supported?

You need Android 8.0 or newer and approximately 24 MB of free storage. Motion-sensor availability varies by device and may affect automatic rep tracking.

How do I update or remove the app?

Install a newer official APK over the existing app to update it. To remove The System, use Android Settings as you would for any other app. Review your account settings separately if you also want to export or delete synchronized data.
