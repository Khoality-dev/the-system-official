FAQ

Before you install.

Why an APK and not the Play Store?

The Play listing is on the way. For now the build ships as a direct APK so early Players can start the climb. Sideloading from a source you trust is standard on Android.

Is it safe to install?

The APK is the same build headed for Google Play. Android verifies every package on install, and the app requests permissions only in context. Always download from this official page.

What's free vs paid?

The entire Body pillar — STR / VIT / AGI, all body quests, stats, leveling, the shop and penalties — is free and offline. Enhanced is a monthly subscription (US$5/mo, regionally priced) that unlocks the Mind pillar: INT / PER, the Mind Gate, and AI-free grading.

Which languages are supported?

English, Vietnamese, Japanese and Korean from day one — including brain-quest content authored per language, not machine-translated.

Do I need an account?

Not for the free Body tier — it works fully offline with no sign-in. Enhanced (the Mind pillar) requires an account by email or Google sign-in, because its grading and sync run online. You can delete your Enhanced account and its data in-app or via a public web link.

What are the requirements?

Android 8.0 or newer, roughly 24 MB, with motion sensors for rep tracking. GPS is optional (Field Gates) and Health Connect is optional (sleep / steps).
