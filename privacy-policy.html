Privacy Policy

Privacy

This policy explains how The System ("the app," "we," or "us") handles information when you use the app. It applies to the Android app and the online services used for accounts and cloud saves.

The short version

The System has no advertising or third-party analytics SDKs, and we do not sell your personal information. Your game progress is stored on your device. If you create an account, the app also sends the information needed to operate that account and synchronize your save to our servers.

Exercise motion data and most Mind-quest answers are processed on your device. The Enhanced subscription's open-ended answers and System briefing are sent through our server to a third-party AI provider only to grade or brief you (detailed below). You can request a copy of your account data or delete your account from the app or our website.

Information we collect

The information we process depends on the features you use:

AccountEmail, date of birth, account ID and sign-in status
DeviceRandom device ID and time zone
ProgressStats, quests, streaks, settings and save history
PaymentSubscription status and billing identifiers for the optional Enhanced subscription — card details are handled by Stripe, not by us

Account credentials. If you sign in with email, your password is sent securely to our account service for authentication. We do not store a readable copy of your password. If Google sign-in is available and you choose it, Google provides an identity token used to authenticate your account.

How we use information

We use this information only to provide and protect the app, including to:

Operate accountsRegister, sign in, verify email and reset passwords
Synchronize progressRestore and merge your save across sessions or devices
Apply accessDetermine which app features your account can use
Prevent abuseMaintain reliable time, sessions and account security

On-device processing

Exercise sensors. While a supported workout is open, the app reads the accelerometer and proximity sensor to count movement. Raw sensor readings are processed in real time and are not stored or uploaded.

Mind quests. Most Mind quests are graded on your device using answer keys included with the app; those answers never leave your device. The Enhanced subscription adds open-ended written questions and a System briefing: when you submit an open-ended answer for grading, your answer text (with the question and, for the briefing, a summary of your in-game progress) is sent through our server to a third-party AI provider (kie.ai) solely to grade that answer or produce that briefing, and to return the result to you. It is used only to generate that response — not to train models — and the AI grades or advises on what you wrote; it never writes your answer for you. Payments are handled separately by Stripe. Completion state may be included in your synchronized save.

Device backup

Android may back up the app's local save so your progress can survive a reinstall or transfer to a new device. Android performs this backup to your Google account's backup storage, subject to your device settings and Google's Privacy Policy. The developer cannot access it. You can disable it at any time in your device's Settings → Google → Backup.

Permissions

The current Android build requests internet access for accounts and cloud synchronization. It does not request access to your contacts, camera, microphone, or precise location. Motion-sensor access does not require a runtime permission prompt. If a future feature requires another permission, the app will request it in context and this policy will be updated before release.

Sharing and sale

We do not sell personal information or use it for targeted advertising. Information may be processed by service providers that help us operate the app — including Stripe (payment processing for the Enhanced subscription), Google (only if you choose Google Sign-In), and our hosting and email-delivery providers. They may process information only to provide those services on our behalf. We may also disclose information when required by law or necessary to protect users, the service, or our legal rights.

Retention and your choices

We retain account information and synchronized progress while your account remains active, and as needed for security, legal, or operational obligations. In the app's account settings, you can export your server-side data or request permanent account deletion. You can also request deletion from our website's account-deletion page without signing in. Deleting the app or your account does not automatically remove copies held in your personal Android backup; those are controlled through your Google backup settings.

Children

The app enforces a minimum age of 16 (or a higher minimum where required in your country) and is not directed to children. Age is checked at sign-up and anyone under the minimum is blocked from creating an account, so we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe an underage person has provided personal information, contact us so we can investigate and delete it.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app changes. When we do, we will revise the date above and provide additional notice in the app when a change materially affects how personal information is handled.
